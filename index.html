IITK Samanvay is thought-leadership platform that brings together visionary minds from industry and academia. Created to bridge the gap between research excellence and real-world application, it enables meaningful dialogue, sparks strategic partnerships, and drives action toward a future shaped by innovation.

Set to take place on 2nd & 3rd September 2025, this year’s edition is themed “Igniting Ideas. Accelerating Innovation.” The event will spotlight collaborative growth and pioneering possibilities across the evolving landscape of innovation, focusing on collaborative growth across cutting-edge technological frontiers.
